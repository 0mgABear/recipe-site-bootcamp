Singaporean Noodles

Prep time: 20 mins

Ingredients

    200 g rice vermicelli noodles
    1 onion
    15 g ginger
    4 cloves garlic
    1 red bell pepper
    4 tbsp soy sauce
    4 tsp fish sauce
    2 tbsp curry powder
    4 tbsp rice vinegar
    1 tbsp sugar
    2 eggs
    1 tsp sesame oil
    10 shrimp
    vegetable oil (for frying)
    cilantro (for garnish)
    scallion (for garnish)
